1. Kadane’s Algorithm
When to use?

👉 Keywords:

maximum sum subarray
maximum profit
best possible sum
largest contiguous sum

👉 Especially when:

array contains negative numbers
no fixed size
no constraint like “sum ≤ k”
💡 Why Kadane?

Sliding window fails with negatives because:

removing left element doesn’t guarantee improvement

Kadane handles this by:
restarting when sum becomes harmful

LC 53 → Maximum Subarray
LC 121 → Best Time to Buy/Sell Stock
LC 918 → Circular subarray sum
LC 152 → Max product subarray (modified Kadane)

❌ When NOT to use Kadane
exact sum = k ❌
count subarrays ❌
constraints like ≤ k ❌
